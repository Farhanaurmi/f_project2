This page was made with the purpose of teaching the coding language of Django, a framework built on Python with the main purpose being web application. You can explore the different tabs of the website and not only learn through the educational content but also the practical functions of they site can be observerd and learnt through, as they also use Django.




Homepage is the on you are visiting right now. Here you can find some general information about the purpose of the different pages of the site and also to reveal some general information about the project.

Learn Django is the educational part of the website, where we have imbedded several videos and also some general introductary information about Django, and also its merits compared to a similar language like PHP.

Clock is simlpy a digital clock, with the purpose of being an easy way of showing the practicallity of even small amounts of code. As making a clock in Django is quite easy, the beginner might start to realize and get interested in the usability of Django.

Visitor is a page where the amount of visitors that have accessed the website shows up. This part connected to our database and counts unique IP as a unique visitors, thus not inflating the visitor numbers from frequent users.

Login/Register is where a user can both register and login, this is stored in our database and makes it easy to see how simple yet useful UI with Django can be.
